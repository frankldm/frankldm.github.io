Some advises for Amir' travel to China

Travel Guide

There is english website for foreigner travellers.
http://www.travelchinaguide.com/

First Stop: Tai An (泰安)

It is famous for the mountain. And I suggest U can climb it if it is possible. But it is very tired.

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tai%27an

1. Travel

This is is website for the mount-tai. which U can find the traffic and price. And there are two options u can reach the peak. 1) climb by yourself 2) by cable car.
But there will be lots lots lots people waiting for cable car, it is normal u need to wait for hours before u can take that cable car.
While u plan to climb the mount, be prepared clothes. it maybe very cold there.

http://www.mount-tai.com.cn/layout_class_service.aspx?id=292

2. Living

There are some hotels, but I am not sure they provide English service. And there is a hostel in Tai’An. According to website, young foreigner travellers recommend there. The location is excellent!!

http://www.yhachina.com/ls.php?id=188&hostID=2

3. Food

I am sure what U can eat and U can’t eat. One advise. It is spicier than it looks. But enjoying the food there.

Second Stop: Su Zhou(苏州)

It is really beautiful city. There is old saying : “Paradise in Heaven; Suzhou and Hanzhou on earth.” and Hanzhou is my hometown.

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suzhou

1. Travel

There are some place U should go. But it dependences how long u will stay there. The
Humble Administrator’s Garden. It is the classical chinese southern garden. Small and delicate.
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Humble_Administrator%27s_Garden

2. Living

Same as Tai’An. There are good hotels there. But it is convenient for foreigner traveller if live in hostel.
http://www.yhachina.com/ls.php?id=117

3. Food

Generally, Chinese food can be roughly divided into eight regional cuisines, which has been widely accepted around. And suzhou is typical Jiangsu cuisine.

Jiangsu Cuisine, also called Huaiyang Cuisine, is popular in the lower reach of the Yangtze River. Aquatics as the main ingredients, it stresses the freshness of materials. Its carving techniques are delicate, of which the melon carving technique is especially well known. Cooking techniques consist of stewing, braising, roasting, simmering, etc. The flavor of Huaiyang Cuisine is light, fresh and sweet and with delicate elegance. Jiangsu cuisine is well known for its careful selection of ingredients, its meticulous preparation methodology, and its not-too-spicy, not-too-bland taste. Since the seasons vary in climate considerably in Jiangsu, the cuisine also varies throughout the year. If the flavor is strong, it isn’t too heavy; if light, not too bland.

My suggestion, eat as much as u can !!!

Last Stop: Shanghai（上海）

It is a huge city, and it changes everyday.

Based on my memory, the following places maybe can represent Shanghai.

  • The Bund


http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Bund

  • Nanjing Road


http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nanjing_Road

  • Lujiazui (The financial centre of shanghai)


http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lujiazui

These three places are close(30 mins by foot between The bund and Nanjing Road), from The Bund to Nanjing Road. U can take the metro.

Living

Food

  • It is sweet. same suggestion. eat as much as u can.

Transportation

Tips

  1. Buy a sim card at the airport. DO ask them to help u enable 3G internet.(Be care of the model of you phone. If u use iPhone. Unicom(联通) will be the better. and tell them u just stay for two weeks, and u want to travel different place. and I think they will give u some good advices.)
  2. Money change. If the time your flight arrived in Beijing is working time. U can change in airport. Just make sure, do not take too much cash, but do have cash with you.
  3. If anything happen, or u lost your wallet. DO call 110(The police number of China, they are willing to help foreigner!!!)
  4. Take an umbrella. In China, we use umbrella more than ducth.LOL

And Enjoy your time in China.
