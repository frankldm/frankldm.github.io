Newton’s Method

The Newton’s Method is the efficient method to look for the zero point of f(x), now we apply this method to find the lowest value of f(x). So we can consider it as the extend algorithm of Newton’s Method.

BE CAREFUL: In wiki, the Newton method is x[k+1] = x[k] - f(x[k])/f’(x[k])
