Recipe

Marinated Beef

Ingredient:

  1. Bay leaf — 3 pieces
  2. Illicium verum — 2
  3. Cinnamon — 1
  4. Sichuan pepper — 10
  5. Sugar — 15g
  6. Soy sauce — 5 Spoon
  7. Beer — 1/4 can
  8. Salt — 1 Spoon

Preparation:

  1. Clean beef shank you purchase from shop, put them with cold water into stainless steel pot and turn gas stove on. Until the water is boiling, then turn off.

  1. Take beef shank out of pot and cooling them for 30min.

  2. Clean the pot and add ingredient[2~8] into, also fill with cold water around half of the pot, also put the cooling beef shank in.

  3. Turn gas stove on and move the button to the biggest fire for 15min, then change to small one for 90min.

  4. Take the beef shank out of pot again, and cooling them again for 30min or more. Then put them back with small fire for 90min.

  5. Done
